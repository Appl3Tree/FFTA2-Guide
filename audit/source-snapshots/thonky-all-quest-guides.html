To complete this quest, go to Camoa to pick up the letter to Meena, then take an airship from Moorabella to Fluorgis, then go to Goug to deliver the letter.

A Paw Full of Feathers

The objective is to defeat all foes. The law forbids using Fire, so when you have your Black Mage attack, don't let him use Fire! If you do end up breaking the law, you won't be able to bring back KOed units, and you won't get as many rewards for completing the Quest.

Please see the full A Request Quest Guide.

A Simple Question

Simply go to Lezaford's house in the Aldanna Range to complete this quest.

Please see the full A Small Favor Quest Guide.

A Step Further

Please see the full A Step Further Quest Guide.

A Treasured Heirloom

The objective is to defeat the Gil Snapper, which is an Adamantitan. Fire, Ice, and Lightning are forbidden here, so be sure that you have not equipped weapons with those elements, and avoid casting magick attacks with those elements.

A Voice from the Well

Please see the full A Voice from the Well Quest Guide.

Abducted!

The objective is to defeat all foes. Actions by Seeq are forbidden, so just don't put any Seeq units into this battle.

Aid the Serpent

The objective is to defeat Duke Snakeheart. Actions by Viera are forbidden, so just don't put any Viera into the battle.

Airship S.O.S.!

The objective is to defeat all foes. Being robbed is forbidden.

All Good Things...

First you have to go to Tramdine Fens to pick up the antidote. Then go to Moorabella to deliver it.

An Earnest Delight

Please see the full An Earnest Delight Quest Guide.

An Earnest Multitude

Accept this quest in Fluorgis. Then walk to the quest location in Fluorgis, and choose the first option in the list. Take an airship to Moorabella and go to the quest location there, and do the same thing. Then walk to Targ Wood and do the same thing.

An Earnest Quandary

When you go to each quest location (Camoa and Graszton), the woman will expect you to dance. Choose the first option in the list each time.

An Earnest Search

Go to Targ Wood and choose the first option in the list.

An Unfamiliar Land

Go to Lezaford's house in the Aldanna Range to complete this quest.

An Unseen Foe

The objective is to protect the Cluckatrices and Crushatrice, and defeat all foes. Doing less than 20 damage is against the law. Don't allow the 'trices to be KOed.

Blue King of Cinquleur

Your objective is to defeat the Blue King of Cinquleur, a level 55 Hume Blue Mage. Using MP is forbidden.

Bonga Bugle - Ashleaf

Your goal is to be the first to reach the tile directly in front of the Galmia P.R. Illusionist. Ranged weapons are forbidden.

Please see the full Bonga Bugle - Blackfrost Quest Guide.

Bonga Bugle - Bloodfire

You will be a shopkeeper, and ten people will come in. For each person, you have to answer with the correct item. The reward that you receive afterward depends on how many correct answers you get.

Bonga Bugle - Mistleaf

This quest is a straightforward battle. The law is against Harming the Weak, but you can break that law without failing the quest.

You will get a reward for completing this quest, and you get a better reward the sooner you complete it.

Cake: The Recipe

You have to check all the sparkles on the ground to complete the quest. Throughout the quest, you will be attacked by a Lamia, a Cockatrice that is weak to Water, an Axebeak, and a Bloody Orb that is weak to Holy.

This quest only becomes available during Silversun. The location of the quest is Camoa.

You have to defeat all foes in three successive battles.

Foodstuffs: Texture

Geomancer's Way - Sun

The objective of this quest is to defeat all foes. The foes are three Geomancers, a Nidhogg, which is weak to Holy, and a Hell Hound.

Gimme That!

In order to accept this quest, you need a Darklord Crystal in your inventory. Then go to the quest location in Zedlei Forest and agree to pay 50 gil.

Goug Cup

Please see the full Goug Cup Quest Guide.

Goug Nightwatch

Your objective is to defeat three Baknamys and a Luchorpan within four rounds.

Graszton Cup

Please see the full Graszton Cup Quest Guide.

Graszton Nightwatch

You must defeat the foes in four rounds. Chita's Weaponers will help you. The law forbids targeting distant units, so don't use Mages, and try to attack units that are backed up against a wall so that you won't break the law if you knockback an enemy unit.

Great Land Festival

Use a Juggler to complete this quest.

Green Dominion

The objective is to defeat all foes. The foes are four Green Mages. Reaction Abilities, such as Counter, are against the law. You can remove Reaction Abilities from your units before the battle by pressing Y to enter the unit configuration screen.

Green King of Cinquleur

The objective is to defeat Green King Verre, who is a Green Mage. There is also a Mimic hiding in the chest, so don't open that chest if you want to avoid fighting it. Restoring HP is against the law.

The reward for this battle is the weapon Save the Queen.

Gripped by Fear

To complete this quest, just go to the Aerodrome in Moorabella, and ask if she is really scared (the first option).

I've Been Had, Kupo!

Your objective is to defeat Nezzel the Alchemist. You can kill or ignore the other enemy units, which are a Black Mage, a Thief, a White Monk, a Green Mage, and a Time Mage.

Inspiration or Perspiration?

Your objective is to protect the guest unit (an Alchemist who does not participate in the battle) and defeat all foes. Your opponents are a Red Marshmallow, Bomb, and Fire Drake. These opponents are all weak to Ice. They will sometimes use Fire attacks to heal each other.

Lord Grayrl!

The objective is to defeat Lord Grayrl. There are also other enemies that you can fight or ignore if you wish. They are two Vikings, a Juggler, and a Thief. The Thief can use Cuisine, which restores an enemy unit's HP completely.

Love-struck

To accept this quest, you will need a Malboro Wine and a Silk Bloom in your inventory. Then just go to the Witch's Hut in Tramdine Fens, and after that, go to the quest location in Moorabella to complete the quest.

Maintaining the Balance

The objective is to defeat all foes. The foes here are five Antlions. They are weak to Air.

This quest is only available in the month of Mistleaf, and only after you have completed Graszton Cup.

You have to fight three consecutive battles, and the objective is to defeat all foes.

Moorabella Nightwatch

The objective is to defeat all foes within four rounds. The foes are three Baknamys and a Luchorpan. There are two guest units: the Bonga Bugle head editor and owner. However, they will not actually do anything during the battle.

The enemies in this quest (which you can ignore if you choose) are Hoppy Bunny, Sprite, Dreamhare, and Deadly Nightshade.

Of Kupos and Cannons

Oh No, Kupo!

This quest becomes available after you have completed For the Cause.

The objective is to defeat the Mamatrice. She can't move, so just go over to her and attack her until the quest is over.

One Last Memory

Ordalia Airships Grounded

The objective is to defeat all foes. There is a Red Marshmallow, Bombs, and a Grenade.

Ordalia Cup

This quest can only be found in the Fluorgis Pub, only during the month of Bloodfire, and only if you have completed Loar Cup.

You have to win five consecutive battles.

Our Playground

The objective is to defeat all foes. There are two Rangers, a Berserker, a Yellow Jelly, a Malboro, and a Grenade.

Pirate Attack

Pirate Problems

The objective is to defeat "Vaan" who is obviously an impostor. The other opponents, which you can ignore if you prefer, are "Penelo" (another impostor), a Cannoneer, a Ravager, a Sniper, and a Fighter.

Plea for Help

Please see the full Plea for Help Quest Guide.

First, go to the quest location in Targ Wood to pick up the quest item. Then go to the quest location in Camoa to drop it off.

Ravager

Please see the full Ravager Quest Guide.

Reagent Run

The objective is to collect a sample of Muskmallow. You will need to check sparkles on the ground until you find the one that is a Muskmallow. You can kill or ignore the Wolves and Dreamhares as you go along.

Red King of Cinquleur

The objective is to defeat the Red Mage. She can use Doublecast, which allows her to cast two magic spells per turn. She is equipped with a Ribbon, so is immune to status ailments.

Rude Awakening

In order to accept this quest, you need to have a Malboro Vine and Foul Liquid in your loot inventory. To complete the quest, first go to the Waterside Hut in Tramdine Fens, then go to Fluorgis and go to the quest location there.

Ruinous Traps

Please see the full Ruinous Traps Quest Guide.

Rumors Abound

Please see the full Rumors Abound Quest Guide.

Seeding the Harvest

The objective is to defeat ten foes. The law forbids Knockback, so you should use magick-users and units that know moves like Air Render, which can't cause knockback. Some of the enemy units in this battle can inflict Silence.

Seeking the Stone

The objective is to defeat all foes. The foes are two Hunters, a Ranger, a Scholar, a Dragoon, and a Paladin. The Scholar knows moves that cause damage to all units, including the enemy units and the Scholar himself.

Shaved Ice

To accept this quest, you need one Ice Stone in your loot inventory. To complete the quest, go to the quest location in Fluorgis.

Shelling Out

The objective of the quest is to uphold the law and defeat all foes. The law forbids using bladed weapons. Certain types of units can only equip bladed weapons, such as Soldiers and Fighters, but you can still use these types of units as long as they don't use their weapons.

Shipping Out

First, go to the quest location in Targ Wood, then go to the quest location in Graszton to complete the quest.

Show of Strength

The objective is to protect Ghi and defeat all foes. Ghi will automatically recover from Disable and Immobilize, so just keep him healed up and attack the enemies until they have been defeated.

Showdown

The objective is to defeat Klesta. Clan Bowen will join the battle.

Sleepless Nights

Please see the full Sleepless Nights Quest Guide.

Sleight of Hand

The objective is to defeat all foes. All of the opponents are Tricksters, which can perform ranged attacks. The law forbids receiving buffs and debuffs. The Tricksters can inflict a number of debuffs.

Something's Dropped!

The objective is to gather all the gemstones. If you kill any enemies, more will spawn, so just finish gathering the gemstones when you are ready to complete the quest.

Speed Battle, Kupo!

This is a speed battle vs. Scarface, so the objective is to make sure that one of your units deals the final blow to the mark, and don't allow the other clan's units to deal the final blow.

Starstruck

Prima Donna will be helping you in this battle. The objective is to defeat all foes.

Stowaways

Please see the full Stowaways Quest Guide.

Stranger in the Woods

Please see the full Stranger in the Woods Quest Guide.

Strong Lady

The objective is to defeat the Night Dancer. You will first need to get past the White Monks who are blocking the path.

Stuck in the Muck

The objective is to find the lost wedding ring. You will need to check on the sparkles on the ground until you find it. You can kill or ignore the enemies that you find here.

Summons

The objective is to defeat all foes. Alys will be a guest unit on your side.

Sun-ripened Mayhem

The objective is to protect the tomatoes and defeat all foes. Don't let any tomatoes die or you will fail the quest. The foes are all Cockatrices.

Survey No. 258

Please see the full Survey No. 258 Quest Guide.

Survey No. 259

Please see the full Survey No. 259 Quest Guide.

Survey No. 260

Please see the full Survey No. 260 Quest Guide.

Survey No. 261

Please see the full Survey No. 261 Quest Guide.

Teach a Man to Fish

The objective is to protect Enchan and defeat all foes. The fisherman will not help in the battle. If he dies, you will fail the quest, so be sure to keep healing him.

Teach a Man to Run

Please see the full Teach a Man to Run Quest Guide.

The Art of Gastronomy

The objective is to defeat all foes.

The Bangaa Brotherhood

To accept this quest, you will need a Fire Stone and a Zirconium in your loot inventory. Then just go to the quest location in Bisga Greenlands to complete the quest.

The Bangaa of the Rupies

The objective is to defeat all foes. The Bangaa Brotherhood clan will fight alongside you, and your opponents are the Nu Mou Nobles.

The Beast of Aisenfield

The objective is to defeat Nesiaam. The terrain here may make it difficult for melee fighters to reach the targets. It can be helpful to bring Gria into this battle so that they can fly to the higher elevations easily. You can also use Faerie Shoes or similar items.

The Camoa Nightwatch

The objective is to defeat all foes within four rounds. The Camoa Braves will be guest units, helping you fight the enemies.

The Cat's Meow

The objective is to protect Roye and defeat all foes. Roye is far away from you when you start, and the law forbids using Ranged Weapons, so be sure to use a unit with high Move and Speed that knows some White Magick so that you can hurry over to Roye and start curing him.

The Dig

The objective is to defeat all foes. There is a Red Chocobo, a Black Chocobo, and four Mooglebanes. The Mooglebanes can cause Charm. You can throw a Knot of Rust at a Charmed unit to remove the Charm status without causing too much damage.

The Eastwatch

The objective is to defeat all foes. The foes are Parivirs, Ninjas, and an Assassin. The Parivirs can inflict Confuse on your units.

The Finest Blade

Please see the full The Finest Blade Quest Guide.

The First Step

To accept this quest, you will need a Cactus Fruit in your loot inventory. Then just go to the quest location in Targ Wood to complete the quest.

The Five Kings

The objective is to defeat all foes, which are the kings of Cinquleur.

The Forests of Loar

The objective of this quest is to visit the specified locations within six days. You should accept this quest in Targ Wood to make sure that you have enough time to visit all of the locations.

The Forgotten Places

Please see the full The Forgotten Places Quest Guide.

The Genuine Article

The objective is to defeat all foes. The foes here are four thieves. Their only abilities are to attack and Steal Gil.

The Goug Consortium

Please see the full The Goug Consortium Quest Guide.

The Honorable Thing

The objective is to defeat all foes. The foes here are a Cannoneer, Defender, Hunter, Parivir, and White Mage.

The Lands of Loar

The objective is to visit the Bisga Greenlands, Baptiste Hill, and the Aldanna Range within five days. Accept this quest in Graszton or Camoa and you should have enough time to complete it within the time limit.

The Last Duelhorn

The objective is to defeat Marquis. He splits into six Ninjas, and you have to defeat them all. They can all inflict status ailments, and all of them have a very high Move stat because they are all equipped with Ninja Tabi, so they can go far on every turn.

The Last Step

To complete this quest, simply go to the quest location in Targ Wood.

The Moon Seal

The objective is to examine the Stone with No Name. There will be some enemies on the field, but you can ignore them if you prefer.

After you touch the Stone with No Name, another battle begins, and the objective is to defeat all foes.

The Natural Order

The objective is to defeat all foes, all of which are Cockatrices. They are all weak to Water.

The Next Step

First go to the quest location in Moorabella to get Clear Sap, then go to the quest location in Targ Wood to complete the quest.

The Nu Mou Nobles

In order to accept this quest, you will need to have a Recall Grass and a Healing Water in your inventory. Then go to the quest location in Baptiste Hill to complete the quest.

The Nu Mou of the Rupies

The objective is to defeat all foes. The foes are the Bangaa Brotherhood. You will be fighting alongside the Nu Mou Nobles.

The Perfect Gift

To accept this quest, you will need a Prima Petal in your loot inventory. Then just go to the quest location in Camoa to complete the quest.

The Ritual

Please see the full The Ritual Quest Guide.

The Rivalry of the Rupies

The objective is to protect Mocedad and Madreth, and defeat all foes. The foes in this case are House Bowen.

The Root of the Problem

The objective of this quest is to defeat all foes. The foes are a Fighter, Ninja, two Thieves, a White Monk, and a Viking.

The Seas of Ordalia

The objective is to visit Tramdine Fens, Aisenfield, and Sant D'alsa Bluff within six days. Accept the quest in Fluorgis and you should have enough time to complete the quest before the time limit.

The Show's Not Over...

In order to accept this quest, you will need a Sweet Sap in your loot inventory. Then just go to the quest location in Fluorgis to complete the quest.

The Shrine of the Paling Gods

Please see the full The Shrine of the Paling Gods Quest Guide.

The Star Seal

The objective is to examine the Stone with No Name. Only one enemy is guarding it, so either ignore the enemy or kill it, then touch the stone.

After that, there is a battle where the objective is to defeat all foes.

The Stone With No Name

Please see the full The Stone With No Name Quest Guide.

The Storage Shed

The objective is to defeat all foes. The foes are a Lanista, Sage, Soldier, and Time Mage.

The Sun Seal

Please see the full The Sun Seal Quest Guide.

The Towns of Loar

The objective is to visit Camoa, Graszton, and Moorabella within seven days. Accept the quest in any of those places, then visit the other two.

The Trappings of Failure

The objective is to destroy all of the traps. You just need to step on them to destroy them. Each trap will reduce your HP by one half.

The Ultimate Book

To complete this quest, just go to Lezaford's cottage in Aldanna Range.

The Way of the Meek

Please see the full The Way of the Meek Quest Guide.

The Way of the Sword

The objective is to defeat all foes, but the only foe here is Ghi, a Parivir. You are only allowed to use Frimelda in this battle. Equip a Ribbon on her.

The Way of the Timid

The objective is to defeat all foes, and the foes are an Archer, a Berserker, a Soldier, a Thief, and a Warrior.

The White of Its Eye

The objective is to protect the white Bloody Orb (Chari) and defeat all foes. The enemies are three Floating Eyes. Be sure to heal Chari as needed.

The Whole Truth

Please see the full The Whole Truth Quest Guide.

The Wonders of Loar

To complete this quest, you have to visit the quest locations in Baptiste Hill, the Aldanna Range, the Rupie Mountains, and the Ruins of Delgantua within 16 days.

The Wonders of Ordalia

To complete this quest, you have to visit the quest locations in Nazan Mines, Sant D'alsa Bluff, and Kthili Sands within 8 days.

The Yellow Wings

The objective is to defeat all foes, and the law forbids using Ice, so don't let your Black Mage use Ice.

Get rid of the White Mage first to stop your enemy from healing.

The Black Mage's spells affect an area, so don't let your units stand close to each other.

Thieves in the Ruins

The objective is to defeat all foes. House Bowen will help you in this quest.

Three-Point Strategy

Please see the full Three-Point Strategy Quest Guide.

Through Another's Eyes

Please see the full Through Another's Eyes Quest Guide.

Throw Down

Please see the full Throw Down Quest Guide.

Time to Act

The objective is to protect the Goug Watch and defeat all foes. You can only choose one of your units. Otherwise the Goug Watch will be doing most of the fighting. Make sure to keep the Moogles healed up, because if any one of them dies, you fail the quest.

To Be a Fighter

The objective is to defeat all foes. The foes are three Fighters, a Soldier, a Thief, and a Warrior.

To Be a Spellblade

The objective is to protect Hana and defeat ten foes. If Hana dies, you fail the quest. Hana will help fight the foes, all of whom are Viera. The final foe is a Spellblade.

To Whom Gods Bow

To complete this quest, just go to Lezaford's cottage in the Aldanna Range.

Training Wanted

The objective is to defeat all foes. The foes are an Animist, a Black Mage, a Fusilier, a Thief, a Tinker, and a Moogle Knight.

Treasured Tomes

To accept this quest, you need one Urutan Annals and one Arnath Glyphs in your inventory. These are both weapons. After accepting the quest, just go to the quest location in Aisenfield to complete the quest.

Tree Hugging

Please see the full Tree Hugging Quest Guide.

Under the Weather

To accept this quest, you need a Potion and a Hi-Potion in your inventory. Then go to the quest location in Tramdine Fens to complete the quest.

Unfamiliar Folk

The objective is to defeat all foes. The foes are an Animist, two Archers, a Black Mage, a Soldier, and a Warrior.

Unplumbed Depths

Please see the full Unplumbed Depths Quest Guide.

Veis, Assassin

Please see the full Veis, Assassin Quest Guide.

Vim, Vigor, and Go

To accept this quest, you need a Crusader Tonic in your loot inventory. To complete the quest, go to the quest location in the Neslowe Passage.

Wall of Flame

Please see the full Wall of Flame Quest Guide.

Wanted: Artillery

Use a Cannoneer or Flintlock to complete this quest.

Wanted: Assistant

Use an Alchemist to complete this quest.

Wanted: Barmaid!

Use a Viera or Gria (any job) to complete this quest.

Wanted: Big Eyes

Please see the full Wanted: Big Eyes Quest Guide.

Wanted: Caretaker

Use a Viera White Mage to complete this quest.

Wanted: Combatants

Use a White Monk or Berserker to complete this quest.

Wanted: Devotees!

At the beginning of the battle, you will need to think back to the hints that you found in the Nightwatch quests. Choose the answers that say "Prima Donna" in them.

The objective is to defeat all foes. The foes are two Baknamys, a Lanista, a Luchorpan, and a Time Mage.

Wanted: Florah

Please see the full Wanted: Florah Quest Guide.

Wanted: Floraxion

Please see the full Wanted: Floraxion Quest Guide.

Wanted: Friends, Kupo!

This quest appears after you complete the game and load the completed saved game. To complete the quest, just go to the quest location in Baptiste Hill.

Wanted: Gaitsnipe

Please see the full Wanted: Gaitsnipe Quest Guide.

Wanted: Gilmunto

The objective is to defeat Gilmunto. The law forbids using Fire.

Wanted: Hatchery Worker

Use a Beastmaster or a Chocobo Knight (not riding a Chocobo) to complete this quest.

Wanted: Icicle Ark

Please see the full Wanted: Icicle Ark Quest Guide.

Wanted: Lang Bros.

The objective is to defeat all foes. The foes are an Archer, a Black Mage, a Fighter, and a Thief.

Wanted: Magick Weapon

Please see the full Wanted: Magick Weapon Quest Guide.

Wanted: Marksman

Use a Fusilier to complete this quest.

Wanted: Moogle Rangers

Please see the full Wanted: Moogle Rangers Quest Guide.

Wanted: Musician!

This quest only becomes available after you have completed the game. Use an Animist or Beastmaster to complete this quest.

Wanted: Shiny Maces

Use an Alchemist, Sage, or Green Mage to complete this quest.

Wanted: Sidekick

Use a Ranger to complete this quest.

Wanted: Sky Pirate Vaan

Please see the full Wanted: Sky Pirate Vaan Quest Guide.

Wanted: The Cyanwolf

The objective is to defeat the Cyanwolf, which is the green wolf. There are also four normal Wolves, but you can ignore them if you prefer. These foes can all inflict Confuse on your units. You can throw a Knot of Rust at a Confused unit to remove the Confuse status.

Wanted: The Mirage Bunny

Please see the full Wanted: The Mirage Bunny Quest Guide.

Wanted: The Mutadragons

Please see the full Wanted: The Mutadragons Quest Guide.

Wanted: Tonberrion

Please see the full Wanted: Tonberrion Quest Guide.

Wanted: Tutor!

Use a Scholar to complete this quest.

Wanted: Ugohr

You only need to defeat Ugohr (the green one) to complete this quest. The law forbids restoring MP, so don't use Ethers.

Wanted: Woodcutter

Use a Viking to complete this quest.

Wanted: Woodworker

Use a Green Mage to complete this quest.

Watch Your Step

Please see the full Watch Your Step Quest Guide.

Watching the Watchers

The objective is to defeat all foes. The foes are a Berserker, Fighter, Master Monk, and Parivir. If the Parivir inflicts Confuse on any of your units, you can throw a Knot of Rust at the Confused unit to remove the Confuse status.

Wayward Drake

The objective is to weaken Goud and defeat all foes. If you kill Goud, you will fail the quest; you have to bring Goud down to HP Critical status without killing him. The foes are Thunder Drakes, a Werewolf, and Goud (a Fire Drake).

What Was Lost

To complete this quest, go to the quest location in Targ Wood and say Galleria Deep. This is the location that Dayvis mentioned in The Way of the Meek.

White King of Cinquleur

Please see the full White King of Cinquleur Quest Guide.

Wish Upon A Star

Please see the full Wish Upon A Star Quest Guide.

Woman of the Wood

The objective is to defeat Ljda, who is an Elementalist. The other enemies, which you can ignore if you choose, are a Banshee, Fire Drake, Ice Drake, Thunder Drake, and White Chocobo.

Yellow Wings in Trouble

The objective is to protect the Yellow Wings and defeat all foes. The foes are House Bowen. If any of the Yellow Wings dies, you will fail the quest.

You Say Tomato

Defeat all foes, but don't use Lightning. Adelle is a Guest unit in this battle.
